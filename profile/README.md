# What is Vulnebify?

**Vulnebify** is a cybersecurity company focused on helping organizations understand how attackers discover and target their external infrastructure.

Our approach is built by experience gained during real-world cyber operations in active conflict environments around the world.

The platform automatically creates an isolated replica of your external attack surface where real attackers interact with realistic decoys. 
This allows us to observe attacker behavior and intent with zero impact on production systems.

Built for threat intelligence teams, red and blue teams, and security-focused developers in highly regulated environments, Vulnebify helps reduce risk by exposing attacker behavior early.

**If it’s online, it’s already being profiled.**
